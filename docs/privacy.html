Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 17, 2026

Nexus Protocol operates as a Local-First Sovereign Node. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any central server.

1. Data Sovereignty

In Phase 1.2, all economic data, transaction logs, and identifiers are processed by your local Sovereign Brain and stored exclusively in the nexus_vault.db file on your machine.

The Body (UI) is a stateless visualization layer. It does not cache or persist your financial data in browser storage.

2. Network & Bridging

By default, Nexus runs entirely on localhost. If you choose to use a bridge (e.g., Ngrok) to access your node remotely, traffic is encrypted in transit. However, you remain responsible for the security of the tunnel you create.

3. Third-Party Services

Nexus Protocol Phase 1.2 does not integrate with external wallets, identity providers, or blockchains. Any interaction with the Telegram API is limited to parsing display names for local rendering only.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about our local-first privacy approach, you may reach out via our official GitHub repository.
