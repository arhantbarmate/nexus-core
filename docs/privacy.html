Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 18, 2026

Nexus Protocol is a Local-First Sovereign Gateway. We do not operate centralized servers to collect or store your data. Privacy is enforced through local execution and cryptographic verification.

1. Multichain Identity (TON & IoTeX)

Nexus handles identity metadata exclusively at the edge.

  • TON: Request signatures (HMAC) are parsed locally. Telegram identifiers are ephemeral and not persisted beyond request verification.
  • IoTeX: In Phase 2.0, device identifiers (ioID) will be used for hardware verification. This data is stored in your local nexus_vault.db and is not accessible by Nexus developers.

2. Off-Chain Compute Readiness

Nexus is architected for W3bstream integration. Off-chain compute does not mean cloud storage; any proofs generated remain under your local control until you explicitly authorize an on-chain anchor.

3. Data Custody

You are the sole custodian of your node's database file. Nexus does not perform behavioral analytics, centralized profiling, or cloud-sync operations.
