Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 15, 2026

Nexus Protocol is committed to your privacy. Unlike traditional applications, Nexus operates as a Local-First Sovereign Node. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any central server.

1. Data Collection & Storage

All economic data, transaction logs, and cryptographic keys are processed and stored exclusively on your device in the nexus_vault.db file. You are the sole custodian of this data.

2. Third-Party Services

In Phase 1.1, Nexus Protocol does not integrate with any wallets or external identity providers. There is no use of TON Connect, no handling of public addresses, and no interaction with blockchain accounts. Any references to wallet connectivity or blockchain identity are strictly forward-looking and planned for future phases of the protocol.

3. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your local vault. However, because the data is stored on your device, the security of your information depends on the security of your local environment (OS, file permissions, and hardware).

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about our local-first privacy approach, you may reach out via our official GitHub repository.
