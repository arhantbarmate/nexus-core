Security Integrity

Hardened Gateway · Fail-Closed Logic
The Fail-Closed Invariant

Nexus operates on a Verify-then-Execute model. If the Sentry Bridge detects an invalid HMAC or corrupted identity context, the system defaults to a 403 Forbidden state, terminating the handshake to protect the Sovereign Vault.

Local-First Isolation

By design, the Nexus Brain is isolated from the public internet. Connectivity is mediated through a single-purpose Sentry Bridge, preventing direct exposure of the nexus_vault.db to public indexing or automated exploits.

Deterministic Economics

The 60/30/10 split is hardcoded within the Brain's core logic. This prevents UI-level manipulation or man-in-the-middle attacks from altering the economic settlement of the node.
