Strategic Displacement

The protocol does not aim to compete with L1 blockchains; it aims to displace the middleware. By moving the logic of economic splits to the edge node, Nexus reduces the trust surface of any platform that integrates it, providing institutional-grade durability on commodity hardware.

"This durability-first validation precedes any identity, mesh, or on-chain settlement features, ensuring architectural correctness before global expansion."
