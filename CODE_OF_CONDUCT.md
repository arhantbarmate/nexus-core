# ⚖️ Code of Conduct — Nexus Protocol

## 1. Our Pledge

As members, contributors, and leaders of the Nexus Protocol project, we pledge to make participation in our community a professional and harassment-free experience for everyone. We are committed to fostering an environment of **Technical Integrity** and **Sovereign Responsibility**.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## 2. Our Standards

### ✅ Positive Behavior
* **Empathy and Professionalism:** Demonstrating kindness and respect toward other contributors.
* **Technical Objectivity:** Focusing on architectural correctness and protocol security over personal preference.
* **Graceful Feedback:** Giving and gracefully accepting constructive peer reviews.
* **Accountability:** Accepting responsibility for mistakes and learning from them to improve the protocol.

### ❌ Unacceptable Behavior
* **Harassment:** Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments and personal attacks.
* **Privacy Violations:** Publishing others' private information (doxing) without explicit permission.
* **Adversarial Conduct:** The use of sexualized language, imagery, or any conduct that undermines the professional standing of the project.
* **Malicious Contributions:** Intentional introduction of vulnerabilities or "backdoors" into the sovereign architecture.

---

## 3. Enforcement Responsibilities

Project leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior. They will take appropriate corrective action in response to any behavior deemed inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.

Project leaders have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and issues that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

---

## 4. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including the GitHub repository, official documentation sites, and any public spaces where an individual is representing the Nexus Protocol.

---

## 5. Enforcement Guidelines

Violations of this Code of Conduct will be met with a tiered enforcement ladder:

1.  **Correction:** A private, written warning providing clarity on the nature of the violation.
2.  **Warning:** A formal warning for repeated or serious incidents, with specific consequences for continued behavior.
3.  **Temporary Ban:** A specified period of suspension from all project interactions.
4.  **Permanent Ban:** For a pattern of repeated violations or severe professional misconduct.

---
